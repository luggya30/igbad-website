Welcome to IGBADI Foundation Uganda

IGBADI Foundation is a non-governmental, politically and religiously neutral, non-profit organisation that addresses the needs of underprivileged members in Kabarole District, Ruwenzori Region, Western Uganda. Since our founding in 2021, we've been working to empower communities to address their own social protection concerns.
