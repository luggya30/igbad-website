Our Programs

Discover how we're making a difference in Kabarole District through our various initiatives

Childcare & Protection

Comprehensive Care for Vulnerable Children

Our childcare program provides holistic support to orphans and vulnerable children in Kabarole District. Since inception, we've cared for 152 children, offering:

  • Basic needs provision (food, clothing, shelter)
  • Psychosocial support and counseling
  • Legal protection and rights advocacy
  • Family reunification and foster care support

Education Support

Access to Quality Education

We work to ensure children and young people in Kabarole District have access to quality education through:

  • School fees and scholastic materials support
  • Advocacy for children's right to education
  • Capacity building for schools and teachers
  • Mentorship and career guidance programs

Economic Empowerment

Strengthening Families & Communities

Our economic empowerment initiatives focus on creating sustainable livelihoods for families through:

  • Vocational skills training for youth and women
  • Microfinance and small business support
  • Agricultural training and inputs provision
  • Linkages to government development programs
